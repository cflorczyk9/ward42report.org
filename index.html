    About this report

    Methodology & disclaimer

    This is an independent, community-built site. It is not operated by the City of Chicago, the Office of the 42nd Ward Alderman, or any city department, and it has no official standing.

    Every figure on this page is pulled live from the City of Chicago's public 311 open data portal at the moment you load it. Nothing is stored on a server, and the only thing saved on your device is a temporary local cache used if the live feed is briefly unavailable. You can view the raw Ward 42 data yourself.

    The 311 dataset has no "closed date" field, so every resolution-time figure on this page is an estimate based on when a request was last updated (last_modified_date). For most requests that update happens when the work is completed, but this is a proxy, not an exact measurement.

    311 measures city service delivery, which ward offices help route, escalate, and expedite on residents' behalf. The underlying work (filling potholes, replacing bulbs, painting over graffiti) is performed by city departments such as CDOT and Streets & Sanitation, not the ward office itself.

    Ward boundaries used for the map's neighborhood filter are approximate bounding boxes, not the official ward shapefile, and are provided for general orientation only.

    Data: City of Chicago 311 Service Requests (dataset v6vf-nfxy), via the Socrata open data API. Report a problem to the city directly at 311.chicago.gov, or visit the 42nd Ward Alderman's office. This page is unaffiliated and independently built.
